About Us

Who are we?

The team behind TMC is composed of high school students who attend Obra D. Tompkins High School in Katy, Texas.


We are the club officers, including the President and Vice President, of our school's Math Honors Society, known as OTHS Mu Alpha Theta, and we are some of the highest skilled students in competitive math in the district.
